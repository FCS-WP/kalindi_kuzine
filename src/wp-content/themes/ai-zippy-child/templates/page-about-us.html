About Us

Established in 2016, Kalindi's Kuzines is a Singapore-based home kitchen specialising in authentic Indian vegetarian cuisine. Prepared fresh daily with quality ingredients and strict hygiene standards. Our dishes reflect the richness of tradition and the comfort of home. Whether for private celebrations or corporate catering, we are committed to serving food that is sincere, wholesome, and thoughtfully prepared.

Rooted in a Home Kitchen

The launching point for Kalindi's Kuzines began in 2016 where a simple idea for experimenting became something more meaningful. What started as a simple dish prepared in a home kitchen grew into much more. It became something more meaningful. What started as a simple idea for sharing became something more meaningful. With each returning order, we built relationships that encouraged us and gave us purpose. We became a trusted source for authentic Indian vegetarian food in Singapore.
