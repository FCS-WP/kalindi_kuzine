Established in 2016, Kalindi's Kuzines is a Singapore-based home kitchen specialising in authentic Indian vegetarian cuisine. Prepared fresh daily with quality ingredients and strict hygiene standards, our dishes reflect the richness of tradition and the comfort of home. Whether for private celebrations or corporate catering, we are committed to serving food that is sincere, wholesome, and thoughtfully prepared.

Rooted in a Home Kitchen

The turning point for Kalindi's Kuzines began in 2016, when a love for cooking became something more meaningful. What started as a simple dish prepared in her home kitchen slowly grew through encouragement from friends and loyal customers.

A Kitchen Built on Care

Every dish is prepared in a 100% pure vegetarian home kitchen, the same kitchen where Kalindi cooks daily meals for her own family. We use quality ingredients, avoid cutting corners, and follow strict hygiene practices.
